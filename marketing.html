What We Do
To Get It Done

  • Website Audit And Development

    One of the most important aspects of digital marketing is your website. We will design and develop a new one or do a complete audit of your current website's foundation/framework to make sure it uses the latest web coding and marketing standards. We'll determine whether or not it employs the latest Search Engine Optimization and Marketing (SEO / SEM) standards, security, browser and device compatibility. We then begin analyzing content to ensure that it communicates your brand effectively, has clear call-to-actions, keeps visitors engaged and active, etc. We take a hard look at your site's analytics to decipher whether you are losing users at key interaction points or not. Last, we ensure your website communicates efficiently with our Marketing Automation Software so we can target and re-engage your growing audience.

    SEE A CASE STUDY

  • Digital Marketing Automation

    We deploy our robust marketing automation software to capture, nurture, and convert inbound leads. Our software creates automated sales funnels and interacts with leads throughout your sales cycle via email, text messaging, social media, and more. It tracks users on your website allowing us to retarget them in the future with similar content. In addition, it begins to learn what your potential customers like or dislike and adjusts its approach accordingly. AgentOptix 360 becomes your automated sales force, intelligently engaging with customers, selling your products and services, and positively impacting your bottom line.

    SEE A CASE STUDY

  • Content Marketing

    Using the latest in media technology and editing techniques, we artfully produce relevant and captivating content that engages your audience, increases website traffic, and makes people want to like and follow your brand on the key social media networks. This content brings brand awareness to potential customers. When they engage, we nurture them to conversion. Well created content such as graphics, images, video, 3D models, blogs etc. that communicates your brand's value proposition effectively, expedites a customer through your sales cycle.

    SEE A CASE STUDY

  • Social Media Marketing and Digital Advertising

    Statistics show that over 80% of the United States population has at least one social media account. These social networks are frequented daily by your future customers. Increasingly, social media is becoming a place where people validate and verify your brand. We manage your social media marketing and engage customers with captivating content. We increase followers and instigate meaningful social interactions and conversations about your brand. Bottom line, our goal is to create not just customers, but brand advocates.

    SEE A CASE STUDY

  • Analytics and Conversion Rate Optimization

    The core marketing objective is to drive qualified traffic to your website and maximize your Return on Investment (ROI). The more traffic you garner the more potential revenue your company can make. We take this a step further by optimizing the probability of conversions. Conversions can range from email sign-ups to online sales, and all are important. We analyze how your website visitors interact with your website's content and scrutinize all available metrics. These include engagement metrics (average time on page and scroll depth), click tracking, bounce rates, referral sites and a host of other website analytics. We do this to optimize your conversion rate so that anonymous website visitors become verifiable leads. This maximizes marketing ROI and leads to growth in revenue.
