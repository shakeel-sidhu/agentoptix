Optix 20/20 features developed to positively impact your bottom line.

  • Section and Page Layouts

    Change every section on the site to create a layout that fits your eye. Show or hide sections of the site with 2 clicks of the mouse. What sometimes costs either time or hundreds of dollars to change is now free and done in seconds.

    Optix 20/20 has the power to allow a user to change all the colors and fonts on the website to fit your existing brand or build a completely new one. Thousands of colors and hundreds of fonts to choose from ensuring your site will be unique.

    Optix 20/20 is built using the latest search engine guidelines.
